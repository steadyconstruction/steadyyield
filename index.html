Key Advantage

Commercial Lease Protection

Unlike standard residential tenancies, our commercial lease model gives you significantly stronger legal protections and far more flexibility as a landlord.

⚖️

Avoid Residential Tenancy Restrictions

In Ireland, residential tenants can acquire long-term rights that make regaining possession difficult. By leasing to us as a company, you avoid these restrictions entirely.

🚪

Flexible Exit Options

Need to sell or move back in? We offer a genuine exit path. Typically just 3–6 months notice depending on the agreement length, and often sooner.

📅

Long-Term Certainty

5 to 10 year lease terms provide you with predictable, stable income over the long run, without the volatility of short-term lets or frequent tenant turnover.

🤝

One Professional Relationship

You deal with us, not individual tenants. One point of contact, one monthly payment, one professional management partner.
